WHO ARE WE?

"Express With Art, Impress With Innovation. "

Bushra Art and Innovation is all about that. We are a dedicated team that is constantly working to make the world more beautiful by contributing whatever we can, and for that we make home decor items, do art installations, sell hand-made paintings, and much more.

End-to-End

From beginning to end, responsibility is ours, so that you can give your time to things which you should.

Quick and Dependable

Meeting the deadline is not our way , beating the deadline sure is. We prefer to do our projects on time to maintain a healthy relationship with our clients.

Futuristic and Advance

We mostly do things using the most advanced technology, like 3D printing and laser cutting, and our innovative team makes things more special with their dedication.

3D Printing & Prototyping

3D printing or additive manufacturing is the construction of a three-dimensional object from a CAD model or a digital 3D model.

Handmade Paintings

Handmade Paintings are made by applying paint, pigment, color or other medium to a solid surface like canvas, paper, cloth, silk, ceramic, wood, glass, pottery, metal or even a wall.

Art Installation

Art Installation an artistic genre of three-dimensional works that are often site-specific and designed to transform the perception of a space

Home Decor

Miniature Modeling

A physical model which is geometrically similar to an object. Scale models are generally smaller than large prototypes such as vehicles, buildings, or people

Laser cutting
